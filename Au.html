AUSTRALIA

Black-Backed Magpie
The black backed magpie is a black bird with white on its lower
back, the middle outer side of the wing, and on the back of
its neck.



Australian Sea Lions
The male Australian sea lion is a chocolate brown color with a
golden-white crown and mane. The females are silvery grey above
and are cream-colored below and on their face.



Southern Hairy-Nosed Wombat
The Southern hairy-nosed wombat has gray-brown fur, a broad blunt
head, and short muscular legs.



Black-Faced Kangaroo
The black-faced kangaroo can be a variety of shades of brown.
Its undersides are pale.



Bat-Eared Fox
The bat-eared fox has large, wide ears, a short, narrow mouth,
and long, jackal-like legs.
