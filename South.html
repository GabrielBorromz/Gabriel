SOUTH AMERICA

Tiger Snake
The tiger snake has a flat body, a short flat head, and large
eyes with vertical yellow pupils. The body colors include
yellow, orange, or reddish-brown. This snake has a dark band
across its neck, a series of dark bands down the back, and
a pale yellow underside.



Brazilian Tapir
The tapir has a short, fleshy trunk formed from the nose and upper
lip. The body is short and stout.



Margay
The margay has a yellow-brown coat with dark spots, tail rings,
and a white belly.



Eleven-Banded Armadillo
The eleven-banded armadillo has a dark brown carapace with
yellowish edges, yellow-gray undersides, and ten to thirteen
move able bands across its back.



Grey Fox
The grey fox has a gray agouti coat, a white throat, and a
black-tipped mane along the top of its tail.
