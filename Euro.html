European Beaver
Although normally reddish-brown, the coat color of the
European beaver can range from yellowish-brown to black.
Its tail is flat and scaly and the hind feet are webbed.



Black-Crowned Night Heron
As its name implies, the black-crowned night heron has a black crown
and mantle. It also has gray wings and white underparts.



Roe Deer Buck
The summer coat of the roe deer is red with a gray face. The chin is
white, and there is a black band from the mouth to the nostrils.
The winter coat is fawn-colored with a white rump patch
and throat. This deer has little to no tail.



Eastern White Pelican
The eastern white pelican has a white body and white upperwing
feathers. The underwing feathers are black and white. The
eastern white pelican has a large, broad bill with a yellow
gular pouch, and its wings have a pinkish tinge during
breeding season.



Black-Winged Stilt
The black-winged stilt is white with all black wings. It has a black cap
to the rear of its head, long pink legs, and a long black bill.
