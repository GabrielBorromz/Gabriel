ASIA

Rose-Ringed Parakeet
The feathers of the rose-ringed parakeet are light green. The males have
a rose-colored ring around their neck. The chin is black, turquoise, and
rose in color, and the central tail feather is blue.



White-Bellied Sea Eagle
The white-bellied sea eagle has a white head and undersides, gray
upperparts, white underwing coverts with black edges, and a white tail.



Common Palm Civet
The common palm civet has a buff to dark brown coat with black stripes
down the back and spots on the sides. It also has a face mask of black
spots and retractile claws.



Eurasian Red Squirrel
The upper coat of the Eurasian red squirrel is uniform, usually deep
brown to red-brown in color. Its ear tufts are visible from the summer
to the winter.



Female Sambar
The sambar has a dark brown coat with light yellow/brown patches
under its chin, inside its limbs, and under its tail.
